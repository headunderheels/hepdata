Fully corrected invariant pi0 yields(*) as a function of pT (GeV/c)
measured in PHENIX EMCal (weighted average PbSc+PbGl) 
for different Au+Au @ 200 GeV centralities.

Columns:
1st: pT (GeV/c)
2nd: error in pT (GeV/c)
3rd: pi0 invariant yield
4th: total error in pi0 inv. yield
rest of columns: See below.

Errors:

eNpi0_stat:     Statistical error
eNpi0_syst:     Systematic error
eNpi0_ccuncorr: Centrality-uncorrelated error (don't cancel out in ratios of diff. centralities)
eNpi0_pTcorr:   pT-correlated error (move all spectrum points up or down together)

(*) Yields are: 1/[2\pi p_{T}N_{evt}] d^{2}N^{\pi^{0}}/dp_{T}dy (GeV/c)^{-2} 
    (invariant pi0 yields per unit rapidity at mid-rapidity).
    To obtain invariant cross-sections, multiply them by \sigma_{AuAu} = 6850 mb

Comments/doubts: David d'Enterria (mailto:denterria@nevis.columbia.edu)
